    Highlanders Boxing Club is a community development corporation to promote human development & physical education by providing people with a sport, physical skill, training, & supportive services that contribute to self-sufficiency. We believe it takes a community to build young men & women into healthy adults. With our mentorship & supportive relationships we hope to provide a gang free environment.
